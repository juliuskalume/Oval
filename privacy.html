Privacy Policy

Last updated April 13, 2026

Oval collects the information needed to create accounts, display profiles, publish opportunities, and operate platform features such as comments, saves, follows, inbox updates, moderation, and account administration.

This includes profile details you provide, account identifiers from your sign-in provider, opportunity data you post, media you upload, and usage signals such as saves, likes, comments, views, follows, and notification state.

How Oval uses information

  • To authenticate users and maintain accounts.
  • To publish, moderate, and display opportunities.
  • To enforce safety rules, including automated and manual review of content and media.
  • To provide inbox updates, profile features, and analytics visible inside the app.

Media and link review

Oval may inspect opportunity links and uploaded media to detect fraud, broken links, sexual content, unsafe material, or policy violations. Media review may use automated moderation tools before content becomes public.

Sharing and third parties

Oval uses infrastructure providers such as Firebase for authentication, database, and storage, and Groq for moderation-related AI analysis when configured. Public profile and post information may be visible to other users and visitors depending on the page and content state.

Retention and deletion

Oval retains account and content data while your account remains active, unless platform rules require earlier removal. You may request account deletion from the app. Deletion requests are reviewed by admins before permanent removal is processed.

Your choices

You can update profile details in settings, control some notification preferences, request account deletion, and choose whether to publish new opportunities. For platform rules, read Terms of Use.
